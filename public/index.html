Frequently asked questions

What is the name of the harbour in Tema, Ghana?

Port Tema.

Where is Port Tema found?

The exact latitude and longitude of Port of Tema are 5.63285° ,0.0127°.

What is the time in Ghana?

GMT 0+.

What is the International Dialling Code for Ghana?

+233

What is the local currency?

The Ghanaian Cedi is the currency of Ghana (Symbol: GHS).

How many airports are there in Ghana and state the distance of the nearest one?

Click here for a list of airports by ICAO code: D#DG - Ghana. The nearest international airport is Kotoka International Airport is 24 km from our Port Tema.

Who handles port operation at Port Tema?

The [INSERTNAME] Corporation Limited (INSERTNAME]) is the only operator for container handling activities at Port Tema and handles also general and bulk cargoes excluding products through pipelines.

How to contact Port Tema?

Port Louis Harbour Radio is the official coast radio for radio communications, manned round the clock throughout the year, through VHF channel 14XXX and channel 16YYY.

Is pilotage service compulsory?

Pilotage service is compulsory for all vessels over 100 GRT and is available round the clock upon request. Conditions apply for white oil and LPG tankers, which are only berthed between sunrise and sunset.

Is outer anchorage possible?

Yes, safe anchorage is available outside the harbour limits in depths varying from 14 metres to 70 metres.
